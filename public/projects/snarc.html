Maximilian Benner

SNARC

Many of nature’s processes can be boiled down to chance and reinforcement; Both processes that had been implemented into machines as early as the 50s. SNARC is an exploration of the human psyche. Through the use of artificial intelligence and constructed environments SNARC questions the nature of our thinking and the construct that is our society. If nature can be summed up as algebra, what distinguishes artificial intelligence from nature?
